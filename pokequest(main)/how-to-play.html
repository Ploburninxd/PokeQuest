📖 How to Play PokéQuest

1. 🌟 Getting Started

Click New Game on the main menu. You will choose one of three starter Pokémon:

🌿 Bulbasaur — Grass/Poison 🔥 Charmander — Fire 💧 Squirtle — Water

Your starter is a gift — it does not count in your Pokédex. Only wild Pokémon you catch are recorded. Once chosen, press Explore to begin!

2. 🌲 Exploring Regions

Each region has its own set of wild Pokémon and a level range. New regions unlock automatically when your strongest Pokémon reaches the required level.

3. ⚔️ Battling Wild Pokémon

When a wild Pokémon appears, choose which of your Pokémon to send out. In battle you can:

⚔️ Move — Opens the move selection screen. Each move shows its type, power, accuracy, and effectiveness against the enemy's type. Always pick super effective moves when you can!

🔄 Switch — Swap to a different Pokémon from your party.

💊 Potion — Heal your Pokémon mid-battle.

🏃 Run — Attempt to escape (50% chance).

💡 Type Effectiveness: Fire beats Grass, Water beats Fire, Grass beats Water — and there are many more type matchups! The move screen always tells you before you commit.

4. 🎣 Catching Pokémon

Weaken the wild Pokémon first — lower HP means a higher catch rate. Then throw a ball:

BallBase Catch RateCost
🟤 Poké Ball30%200 credits
🔵 Great Ball50%500 credits
🟡 Ultra Ball70%1,000 credits
💜 Master Ball100%5,000 credits
💡 Pro Tip: Get the enemy below 10% HP for a near-guaranteed catch! Watch the "Catch: X%" indicator on the battle screen.

If your party is full (6 Pokémon), caught Pokémon are automatically sent to your PC Box.

5. 📈 Levelling, Moves & Evolution

Your Pokémon gain XP from battles and catches. When XP reaches the threshold (level × 50), they level up — gaining HP, Attack, and sometimes new moves. At set levels, Pokémon evolve into stronger forms automatically.

Each Pokémon can know up to 4 moves. If a new move is available and you already have 4, you choose which one to forget. You can also teach moves using 📀 TMs bought from the shop — open a Pokémon's details and press Use TM.

Use a 🍬 Rare Candy to instantly level up any Pokémon by one level.

6. 🏆 Gym Battles

Each gym region has a Leader with a type-specialist team. Press 🏆 Gym to challenge them. The gym battle works exactly like wild battles — use the Move button, pick type-effective attacks, and defeat all their Pokémon to earn a badge and credits.

There are 8 badges to collect: Boulder, Cascade, Thunder, Rainbow, Soul, Volcano, Marsh, and Champion. Collect all 8 to prove you are the Pokémon Champion!

💡 Strategy: Check the gym's type specialty (shown on the selection screen) and bring Pokémon with super effective moves. Use TMs to fill any type coverage gaps.

7. 🖥️ PC Box & Party Management

Your party holds up to 6 Pokémon. Use 🖥️ PC Box to deposit extras into unlimited storage and withdraw them any time. This lets you build specialist teams for different regions and gyms.

8. 🤝 Buddy System & 🏅 Achievements

Open any Pokémon's details and tap Make Buddy to set a walking partner. As you explore, your buddy gains happiness, unlocking bonuses. There are 18 achievements to unlock — from your first catch to collecting all 8 badges. Check your progress any time with the 🏆 Achievements button.

9. 💾 Saving Your Game

Click 💾 Save to download a save file. Click 📂 Load to restore it. Your game auto-saves to the browser every 60 seconds, but always save a file before closing. Use 🔄 Reset to start fresh — this cannot be undone!
